Pet Grooming

At Grey Standard, grooming is a calm, detailed process focused on comfort, hygiene, and style
A basic groom takes up to 2 hours, while a full grooming session may take up to 4- 5 hours, depending on coat type, size, and condition

1. Haircut / Styling

A professional, breed-appropriate or coat-condition–based haircut designed to enhance the pet's natural structure, comfort, and overall appearance

2. Bath

Two-step bath using quality shampoo and conditioner to cleanse and nourish the coat

3. Blow Dry

High-velocity drying for a clean, fluffy, and tangle-free finish

4. Brush Out

Gentle brushing to remove loose hair and prevent matting

5. Nail Trim

Safe trimming to maintain healthy paw posture

6. Ear Cleaning

Cleaning to remove dirt and reduce odor

7. Paw Pad Trim

Trimming excess hair for better grip and hygiene

8. Anal Gland Expression

Involves manually expressing fluid from small sacs near a Grey Standard pet's anus

9. Sanitary Shave

Hygiene-focused trimming around sensitive areas

Services

All additional services are performed only in combination with a Basic or Full Grooming package • Individual services are not available separately, as we prioritize complete care, hygiene, and your pet's overall wellbeing

1. Medicated Bath

A therapeutic bath using vet-approved products to help with specific skin or coat conditions

2. Tick & Flea Treatment

Targeted treatment to remove ticks and fleas and help protect the coat from re-infestation

3. Deshedding

A specialized process to reduce excessive shedding by removing loose undercoat and dead hair

4. Dematting

Careful removal of knots and mats to improve comfort and coat health. Performed gently to avoid stress

5. Light Trimming

A short haircut session focusing only on essential areas such as the face, paws, sanitary zones, or minor coat adjustments, when a full groom is not required
